## Budget Considerations
Most tactics above are **free** (time investment only):
- Content creation: DIY
- Search Console: Free
- Directory submissions: Free
- Community engagement: Free
- PR outreach: Free (time)

**Optional Paid Tactics (Low Budget):**
- Ahrefs/SEMrush subscription: €100/month (keyword research, backlink monitoring)
- Sponsored content: €500-1000 per article (TheJournal.ie, etc.)
- Google Ads: €200-500/month (complement organic while building)

**Recommendation:** Focus on free tactics first. Site has strong technical foundation and unique data—just needs content and promotion.
